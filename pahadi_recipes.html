1. Aloo Ke Gutke

Aloo Ke Gutke is a traditional Pahadi dish made with boiled potatoes, spices, and mustard oil. It is typically served with Mandua (finger millet) or Bhat (rice) Rotis.

2. Kafuli

Kafuli is a popular Pahadi dish made from spinach and fenugreek leaves cooked with gram flour and flavored with spices. It is a nutritious and delicious curry served with steamed rice.

3. Bhatt Ki Churdkani

Bhatt Ki Churdkani is a traditional Uttarakhandi recipe made from black soybeans. The beans are soaked overnight, boiled, and then cooked with spices and ghee, resulting in a rich and flavorful dish.
