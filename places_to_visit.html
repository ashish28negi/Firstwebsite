10 Places to Visit in Uttarakhand

Home Places to Visit

1. Nainital

Nestled in the Kumaon region, Nainital is a picturesque hill station known for its beautiful Naini Lake, lush greenery, and pleasant weather. It offers boating, trekking, and stunning views from Naina Peak.

2. Mussoorie

Popularly known as the "Queen of Hills," Mussoorie is a charming hill station with breathtaking views of the Doon Valley. Enjoy the Mall Road, Kempty Falls, and trek to Lal Tibba for a panoramic view.

3. Jim Corbett National Park

India's oldest national park, Jim Corbett, is famous for its tiger reserve and diverse wildlife. Go on safaris to spot tigers, elephants, leopards, and enjoy the serene beauty of the surrounding landscape.
